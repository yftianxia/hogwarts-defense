## Bugs Found

### BUG-0001 — S2: Continuous contact damage neutralized by HP rounding

**Severity**: S2 (Major — broken gameplay feature)
**System**: Combat / Player Damage
**Source**: `src/index.html` line 863

**Description**:
Line 863 rounds the player's HP to the nearest integer every frame:
```javascript
player.hp = Math.round(Math.max(0, Math.min(player.maxHp, player.hp)));
```
Continuous contact damage from enemies is applied per-frame as `enemy.damage * dt` (line 762). At 60 FPS with typical delta times:
- Apprentice: `8 * 0.0167 = 0.133` damage/frame
- Elite: `15 * 0.0167 = 0.25` damage/frame  
- Acromantula: `25 * 0.0167 = 0.417` damage/frame

Since these values are all below 0.5, `Math.round()` rounds the HP back to its previous integer value every single frame — **the damage is erased**. For example:

1. Frame N: HP = 100 (integer). Damage = 0.133. New HP = 99.867. Rounded: 100.
2. Frame N+1: HP = 100 (it was set to 100). Damage = 0.133. New HP = 99.867. Rounded: 100.
3. Repeat forever. Player takes **zero effective damage** from enemy contact.

Contact damage only registers when cumulative per-frame damage exceeds 0.5 in a single frame (e.g., 4+ apprentices at once), or when combined with an enemy projectile hit in the same frame.

**Reproduction**: Stand next to a single apprentice enemy and maintain overlap. Observe player HP never decreases.
**Fix**: Remove `Math.round()` from the HP mutation. Round only for display:
```javascript
document.getElementById('hp-display').textContent = Math.round(player.hp);
player.hp = Math.max(0, Math.min(player.maxHp, player.hp));
```
Also affects enemy projectile damage from elites (7.5 damage gets rounded to 7 or 8 depending on current fractional HP) and acromantulas (12.5 damage).

### BUG-0003 — S3: Missing hit/explosion SFX on spell kills

**Severity**: S3 (Minor — cosmetic/feedback)
**System**: Audio
**Source**: `src/index.html` lines 410-412 (Q) and 690-691 (R fire wall)

**Description**: When Q or R (fire wall) kills an enemy, visual particles play but `sfxHit()` and `sfxExplosion()` are not called. Only auto-attack projectile kills produce the hit/explosion sounds. This creates inconsistent audio feedback.
**Fix**: Add `sfxHit(); sfxExplosion();` calls at lines 411 (Q kill path) and 691 (fire wall kill path).

### BUG-0004 — S4: shootTimer frozen during CC (design quirk)

**Severity**: S4 (Trivial — design choice)
**System**: Enemy AI
**Source**: `src/index.html` lines 722 and 742

**Description**: `enemy.shootTimer -= dt` at line 742 is skipped when the enemy is stunned or frozen (due to `continue` at line 722). The timer resumes from its paused value after CC expires, rather than continuing to tick down. Most action games allow timers to tick during CC so enemies attack immediately on release if the timer expired.
**Fix**: Move `enemy.shootTimer -= dt` above the `continue` statement (before line 722) if the timer should tick during CC. Otherwise, document as intentional.

---

### BUG-0005 — S4: Fractional overkill damage recorded

**Severity**: S4 (Trivial — stats accuracy)
**System**: Damage Stats
**Source**: `src/index.html` line 406, line 684

**Description**: When Q deals 45 damage to an enemy with only 10 HP remaining, the full 45 is recorded to `damageStats.q`. Similarly, fire wall damage records the full `damagePerSec * dt` per tick even on the killing tick. This means damage stats overcount relative to actual HP removed.
**Fix**: Cap recorded damage to `Math.min(damage, enemy.hp)` before recording. S4 — only relevant if damage stats are used for balance tuning or achievements.
